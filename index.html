Khublei

Simple Greeting Card Making App,
Make Cards and Share it with your friends.

This is a simple greeting card maker app.
Khublei, A Khasi word which means to Greet. It also means Thank You.
The word is said to be derived from two words, Kyrkhu and Blei which when translated to English means God Bless.

It is an ad-free and open source app build as a group project by the students taking Android Development Course, in 2019 with the guidance of Mr. Kitborlang L. Kynshi and Mr. Kyrshanlang R. Dkhar.
